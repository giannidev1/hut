# Hut Brand Colors

## Primary Palette

### Primary Orange
- **#E46A45** - Bright coral/orange (main brand color)
- **#E25741** - Coral/orange-red (accent)
- **#E58348** - Medium orange

### Secondary Warm
- **#FAAF52** - Warm orange/yellow (highlights)

### Neutrals
- **#F5E9D8** - Light cream/beige (backgrounds)
- **#E0DDCC** - Light beige (alternate background)

### Earthy Accents
- **#7D792F** - Olive/khaki green (accent)
- **#6A4628** - Brown (text/dark elements)
- **#3A2D27** - Dark brown (primary text)

## Usage Guidelines

### Suggested Mapping
- **Primary Brand Color**: #E46A45
- **Secondary/Highlights**: #FAAF52
- **Backgrounds**: #F5E9D8, #E0DDCC
- **Text (Dark)**: #3A2D27
- **Text (Medium)**: #6A4628
- **Accent Colors**: #E25741, #E58348, #7D792F
